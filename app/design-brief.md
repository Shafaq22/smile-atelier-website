# Design brief: Luxury Smile Atelier
## Design read
For elite cosmetic dentistry clients seeking the absolute height of smile artistry, conveyed through a hushed, ultra-premium, digital experience.

## Concept spine
"The Jewel Atelier" — Treating veneers not as dental appliances, but as custom-crafted gem commissions, presented in museum-like darkness.

## Locked palette
Ground: #0D0C0B / Surface: #171512 / Text ink: #F5EFE3 / Muted text: #A79E8E / ACCENT (only one): #C8A96A
Defense: The deep onyx black grounds and champagne gold accents evoke a high-end luxury jewelry house, framing the glowing white porcelain veneers.

## Locked type
Display: Satoshi (500/700/800) + Body: Inter Tight (400). No third font.

## Hero technique
Technique B: Canvas Frame Scrub. Scroll plays the veneer awakening film frame-by-frame, enacting the "crafted sculpture" spine.

## Section plan
1. Section 1 (Hero) — Layout: pinned film (B)
2. Section 2 (Proof strip) — Layout: facts + hairline dividers
3. Section 3 (Space chapter) — Layout: sticky film chapter
4. Section 5 (Offerings) — Layout: icon rows + click-to-expand
5. Section 6 (Technology) — Layout: split copy/film
6. Section 8 (Team) — Layout: film band + divide-y rows
7. Section 11 (Quote) — Layout: oversized statement
8. Section 15 (FAQ) — Layout: plain accordion
9. Section 18 (Visit finale) — Layout: full-bleed film + info card
